

# Plan: Terms of Service — Alignment & Risk Pass

## Summary

Complete rewrite of the Terms of Service to address significant legal risks, align with product reality, and shift from "intent policing" to proper liability framing. The current terms contain legally dangerous language that attempts to regulate user intent and offline behavior—this pass fixes that.

**Replace current Section 2 entirely.** This is the most important legal change.

```text
tools to help users explore real-world locations and experiences. The platform 
is designed specifically for gay men who enjoy outdoor and active lifestyles.

ThickTimber does not:
• Arrange, organize, or facilitate meetings between users
• Verify the identity, intent, background, or conduct of users
• Supervise, monitor, or participate in offline interactions
• Guarantee any outcomes, connections, or experiences

Any interaction between users—online or offline—is entirely voluntary and 
occurs at the users' sole discretion. ThickTimber is not responsible for 
the conduct of any user, whether on or off the platform.
```

**Why this matters**: This single section does the legal heavy lifting—it defines the platform as an information service, not a matchmaker, and shifts responsibility for interactions to users.

## Legal Strategy Summary

### Before (Risky)
- Polices user intent ("not for dating")
- Claims control over offline behavior
- Uses moralistic language ("platonic only")
- Missing liability protections

### After (Protected)
- Defines platform as information service
- Shifts responsibility to users
- Assumption of risk for outdoor activities
- Strong disclaimers and indemnification
- No attempt to regulate offline outcomes

---

## Guardrails Applied

- Removed all intent-policing language
- Removed "platonic/friendship-only" framing
- Added proper outdoor activity risk disclaimers
- Added user interaction disclaimers
- Added 18+ content framework
- Added professional advice disclaimer
- Added indemnification clause
- Added governing law clause
- Consistent with product features (couples, PRO, 18+ community)

---

## Net Result

The Terms now:
- Match product reality (couples, 18+ content, outdoor focus)
- Shift liability appropriately to users
- Stop trying to regulate intent or offline behavior
- Provide proper outdoor activity protection
- Are enforceable and legally sound
- Remain future-safe for additional features
