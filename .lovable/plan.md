## Changes Required

### 1. Add "No Promotion Required" Statement

**Location:** "How Trail Blazers Contribute" section (after bullet list, line ~236)

**Current:**
```text
All submissions are reviewed to ensure they add value and align with the platform's purpose.
```

**Updated:**
```text
All submissions are reviewed to ensure they add value and align with the platform's purpose.

Participation does not require promotion, backlinks, or ongoing contribution.
```

**Why:** Removes silent friction for bloggers/writers who worry about quid pro quo obligations.

---

### 2. Add Prestige Signal Line

**Location:** "For Writers, Guides & Experts" section (end, after line 276)

**Current:**
```text
Trail Blazer links are selected based on relevance and quality—not reach or follower count.
```

**Add after:**
```text
Trail Blazer contributions are selected for relevance and experience with this type of place.
```

**Why:** Signals editorial taste and attracts higher-quality contributors without creating ego dynamics.

---

### 3. Strengthen Editorial Standards Language

**Location:** "What Are Trail Blazers?" section (line ~175)

**Current:**
```text
This program exists to highlight expertise, not influence.
```

**Updated:**
```text
This program exists to highlight expertise, not influence. All contributions are reviewed for quality over quantity.
```

**Why:** Reinforces editorial curation without mentioning credentials, bios, or rankings.

---

### 4. Application Expectation Line

**Location:** Application form section (line 391)

**Current:**
```text
Submissions are reviewed for relevance and quality. There is no requirement to focus on a specific location or audience.
```

**Updated:**
```text
Applications are reviewed for quality, relevance, and intent. There is no requirement to focus on a specific location or audience.
```

**Why:** Prepares applicants for review and reduces entitlement mindset.
